Interactive

This is a 50s themed room that I 3D modeled and used Unity to activate and animate. I used unity to make it so that when exist in the space, you can hear sound from the tv, phonograph, and radio, but upon viewing any of the animated or sound playing elements of the scene, they freeze and are silenced.
